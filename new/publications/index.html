2024

  1. Look around and learn: self-improving object detection by exploration
    Gianluca Scarpellini, Stefano Rosa, Pietro Morerio , and 2 more authors
    ECCV 2024, 2024
    Abs arXiv

    We teach an embodied agent to look for disagreement in detected objects, in order to collect samples for fine-tuning an off-the-shelf detector. We analyze the zero-shot transfer of the learned policy.

  2. Mind the error! detection and localization of instruction errors in vision-and-language navigation
    Francesco Taioli, Stefano Rosa, Alberto Castellini , and 5 more authors
    IROS 2024, 2024
    Abs arXiv Code Website

    We propose a benchmark that introduces instruction errors in VLN datasets and a baseline method for detection and localization of errors.

  3. I2EDL: Interactive Instruction Error Detection and Localization
    Francesco Taioli, Stefano Rosa, Alberto Castellini , and 5 more authors
    RO-MAN, 2024, 2024
    Abs arXiv

    We extend IEDL to programmatically interact with the user, balancing interactions and success rate increase.

  4. Tour guide robot: a 5G-enabled robot museum guide
    Stefano Rosa, Marco Randazzo, Ettore Landini , and 4 more authors
    Frontiers in Robotics and AI, 2024
    Abs PDF Code

    We present development choices and findings in the deployment of a 5G-connected autonomous tour guide robot.
